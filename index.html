AKAN-NAME GENERATOR

In Ghanian culture, it is a common occurrence for children to have Akan Names.You may be wondering what an Akan name is.Well, Akan Names or "day-names" are names given to the Akan people's children based on the day of the week that a child was born. They often have a deeper meaning regarding the child's character and soul. Akan names are also gender specific. This means that although the inherent meaning of the name is the same, a male and female child born on the same day will have different "day-names"

Common Akan names and their associations :
DAY MALE FEMALE ASSOCIATION
Sunday Kwasi Akosua Universe

Friday Kofi Afua Fertility
Saturday Kwame Ama God
Some prominent Ghanian personalities with Akan names are:
  • Kofi Atta Annan - former United Nations Secretary-General.
  • Kwame Nkrumah - Ghana's first president.
  • Adwoa Caitlin Maria Aboah - popular Ghanaian-British fashion model.
